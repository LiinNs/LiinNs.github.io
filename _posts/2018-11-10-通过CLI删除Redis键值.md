### 批量删除Redis Key-Value

1. Windows系统下   
`./redis-cli.exe -a mypassword -h 127.0.0.1 -p 6379 -n 6 eval "redis.call('del', unpack(redis.call('keys','KEY*')))" 0`  
<!-- more -->
2. *nux 系统下  
`redis-cli keys "*" | xargs redis-cli del`  
`redis-cli keys "*"|xargs -I {} redis-cli del {}`
